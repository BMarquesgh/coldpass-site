Support

Most questions are answered right here.

ColdPass has no account and stores nothing on a server, so most issues are resolved on your own device using the steps below.

Getting started

  • On first launch, ColdPass creates your vault: generate a recovery phrase, confirm it, and set a biometric unlock. There is no account to create.
  • Tap Add service to add a login. ColdPass derives a strong, unique password for it on your device.
  • Tap an entry to reveal or copy its password — protected by Face ID / Touch ID.

Turning on AutoFill (iOS)

  • Open the iOS Settings app → General → AutoFill & Passwords.
  • Enable ColdPass as an AutoFill provider.
  • On any login screen, tap the password field and choose ColdPass from the keyboard bar. Fills are gated by Face ID / Touch ID.

Frequently asked questions

Where are my passwords stored?

They aren't. ColdPass derives each password on demand from inputs you control and never saves the result. There is no server and no cloud copy.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Install ColdPass on a new device and restore from your recovery phrase. Everything is regenerated. This is why keeping your recovery phrase safe is essential.

I lost my recovery phrase. Can you recover my vault?

No. ColdPass is self-custodial — there is no password reset and no way for anyone, including us, to recover your vault without the recovery phrase. This is the trade-off for having no company hold your data.

Does ColdPass send my data anywhere?

No. The app makes no network requests to operate your vault, and it contains no analytics or tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

Can I use ColdPass for two-factor (2FA) codes?

Yes. You can store TOTP secrets alongside your logins and ColdPass generates the codes on-device, fully offline.

What is the duress vault?

An optional second PIN opens a separate decoy vault. Under pressure you can reveal the duress PIN while your real vault stays hidden. Optional panic-wipe and canary tripwires add further tamper protection.

Still need help?

Because ColdPass keeps everything on your device and has no account, the steps and answers above resolve the vast majority of questions. New to the app? Start with How it works.
