Passwords you never store.

ColdPass is a self-custodial password vault built on the security model of a crypto hardware wallet. No cloud, no account, no server to breach — your passwords are derived on your device, on demand.

Get started Currently in TestFlight beta for iOS.

Why ColdPass is different

Most password managers encrypt your vault and upload it to their servers. ColdPass doesn't store passwords at all.

No servers, no cloud

There's no backend and no account. Nothing to breach, subpoena, or leak.

Derived, not stored

Each password is recomputed on demand from inputs only you control — never written down.

You hold the keys

A single recovery phrase regenerates everything on a new device. Self-custody, like a hardware wallet.

AutoFill + Face ID

Fill logins in Safari, browsers, and apps — gated by Face ID or Touch ID every time.

Built-in 2FA

Keep your TOTP codes next to your logins. One app instead of two.

You choose the crypto

Pick the key-derivation function, hash, length, and character set per entry. No hidden defaults.

Duress & tripwires

A second PIN opens a convincing decoy vault. Panic wipe and canary tripwires detect tampering.

Quantum-ready

Optional hybrid post-quantum protection for your most long-lived secrets and sharing.

How it works

No sign-up. You're in control from the first launch.

Create your vault

Generate a recovery phrase on your device and set a biometric unlock. No email, no account.

Add your logins

For each service, ColdPass derives a strong, unique password on demand — it's never stored.

Fill anywhere

Use AutoFill across apps and the web, unlocked by Face ID. Your data never leaves the device.

Self-custodial means no recovery. Your recovery phrase is the only way to restore your vault — there is no password reset and no support team that can recover it. Back up your phrase safely.
